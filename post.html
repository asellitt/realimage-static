Internet/Editorial

Quality and luxury combine to create this exceptional brand new family home, positioned in an exclusive Glen Waverley locale amid a choice of quality public/private schools. Artfully constructed by Eashara Homes, the opulent 68 square residence features breathtaking 3 metre ceilings, gleaming timber floors, and a soft neutral décor throughout.

A grand double entrance leads inside to the spacious formal lounge, and beyond to the oversized kitchen/dining/family zone. The exquisite marble kitchen boasts Miele appliances, double oven, soft-closing cabinetry, and a fully equipped butler’s pantry like no other. The 5 luxury bedrooms all enjoy walk-in robes and sleek ensuites (the master with a robed dressing room and spa ensuite complete with television). Further living spaces include a luxury home theatre, vast upstairs retreat, 2 studies, and billiard room serviced by a generous bar/store.

A covered alfresco area provides a wonderful indoor/outdoor space complete with outdoor kitchen and views across the manicured lawn. Finishing touches include ducted heating/refrigerative cooling, ducted vacuum, home automation, security alarm, intercom, and integrated remote double garage accessed via auto gates.

With easy access to a plethora of restaurants, cafes, bus, train, and quality shopping precincts – you will quite simply want for nothing.
